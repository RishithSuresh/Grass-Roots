About GrassRoots

GrassRoots connects smallholder farmers with markets and buyers using transparent tools, traceability, and locally-accessible services. We combine simple call-in workflows, QR traceability, and direct market access to improve farmer incomes and product trust.

  • Mission: Empower farmers with practical digital tools and fair market access.
  • Approach: Low-barrier tech (voice, QR) + market linkages + community support.
