About GrassRoots

We help farmers access markets, record provenance, and get fair prices by combining low-barrier technology with local partnerships.

Our Mission

To increase smallholder farmer incomes and market visibility through practical tools that respect local languages and workflows.

How We Work

Voice-first tools

Farmers can submit crop details via simple call-in systems in their local language.

Traceability

QR-based tracking helps buyers and consumers verify product origin and quality.

Market Linkages

We connect farmer groups directly with buyers to improve margins.
